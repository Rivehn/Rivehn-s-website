In 2019 Ivan proposed to the house a project for the year to which we as computer science students, develop a new game. This never occurred until now, I, Peter Deng, have a dream. I would like to build a new game (by my own) with the collective sourcing of help from the original Kitchener 6.

method of development agile

The following Agile format is proposed by Peter Deng

-          Idea I would like to list ideas, get opinions from others to cement a good decent idea. If there are no good opinions, I think Ill just make an RPG, I like RPGs.

-          Requirements Such a weird word, I prefer to just look at tasks and priorities together (using MoSCoW). From the idea I will do a tree priority list of requirements and work from there. Also, Source Control.

-          Sprint plan a continuous sprint plan will be made to organise the tasks.

o   Scrum meetings will be ninja done with the members in discord without them knowing.

-          Testing will occur at the end of each sprint when I am bothered. I then discuss what they like, what they didnt like and what should be changed.

ABOUT ME

Video games, Gameplay design, Art, Music, Sport, Film

I am a student at the University of Southampton studying Computer science in my second year. My main interests lie in games. My favourite types of games are competitive and strategic games, puzzle and logic games, rpgs and bullet hells. Outside of video games, I aspire to be a game designer and developer, mostly looking to a flexible developer that wishes to create games that are small and large. I enjoy studying gameplay and game design choices. I try to analyse and critique the design choices of various games in this blog hoping people can share my insights and discuss with me about these things as they are very fun to talk about for me. I thoroughly enjoy looking at art of all kinds, I think part of making art is to be appreciated so I aspire to always appreciate the art that someone else produces great or small. I do not often watch films as I would like to because I feel the modern era of films has left a lot to be desired and that they have regressed slowly from an artform. That being said there are always gems that I hope to catch, I will be discussing my thoughts, ramblings and informal reviews on here too.

On a more personal scale, I grew up fairly sheltered and scared to meet new people but eventually grew out of it when I entered past my adolescence, I'm still a bit of a scaredy cat however, that hasn't changed ^^. I think of myself as a bit of a tradionalist, I am very grounded in each person respecting their heritage and taking pride no matter where they come from. I also think with an egoistic and individual mindset because I agree the direction of our lives is steered by our choices, and no matter what influences your choices we are still the ones who ultimately make the final decisions. My hobby is studying philosophy with my favourite areas being existentialism, eastern philosophies, and analytical philosophy. My favourite animals in order are Rabbits, Dogs and horses. I was born with rabbit as my zodiac, I feel a deep connection to it and it means a lot to me so much so that I would even call it destiny that I was born with this sign,, in easter no less and on the following week to good friday. I even like gazing at the moon!

I am intending to use this for my game ideas mostly but also for lots of other interesting things I want to talk about. For example, I actually quite like romance novels, but the majority of novels I have currently read are classical novels from before the 20th century. Classical novels feel like they are much better written than modern ones, but maybe it's because of the intended audiences, modern writers must write in colloquial ways that disappoint me. I really like the silver tongue text that classical novels have, there is a bit of elegance in the way they were wrote which I adore. In regards to my game ideas, I have a whole universe planned out that is my one and only dream, to create and share with everyone. The name of this world is Crowlion, and I wish for it to grow into the gem that the kid all those years ago dream't it would grow to be. It's funny how the aspirations and desires of adults usually ends up being little dreams of a growing child. That's all I am going to disclose for now about Crowlion. I plan on unveiling the machinations of the world through novels and games that I am working hard to come to life. I guess finally, the last thing I would like to say is, for those that are reading this, I am truly grateful that you were able to see a side of me I do not show to other's, because if you are reading this out of the curiousity of your heart and not by the frivolent pleads of a lost boy lost in his world, it means I made my dream come true, a dream not of my older self, but a dream of my younger self, for that is the only wish that my older self has, the happiness of that child all those years ago.

Peter Deng/Rivehn
